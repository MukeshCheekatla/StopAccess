# FocusGate 2026 — FAQ & System Reference

## General Questions

### How does the Hybrid Sync Strategy work?
FocusGate uses a multi-layered approach to blocking.
1.  **Browser Shield**: Intercepts requests directly in your browser using Chrome's Declarative Net Request (DNR) API. This is instant and works without a network connection.
2.  **Cloud Guardian**: Synchronizes your rules with NextDNS. This ensures that if you add a block on your laptop, your Android phone and secondary devices are also protected at the network level.
3.  **App Engine (Android)**: Monitors screen time locally on your device and applies limits. If a limit is reached, it signals the Cloud Guardian to block the app's network domains.

### What is Strict Mode?
Strict Mode is designed to prevent impulsive unblocking. When enabled, any attempt to disable protection, delete a rule, or extend a limit requires a **behavioral friction cooldown** (usually 5–60 seconds). This short pause is often enough to break the cycle of distraction and help you stay committed to your focus goals.

### What is the Guardian PIN?
The Guardian PIN is an optional 4-digit code that locks critical settings. It is recommended to have a trusted friend or family member set this PIN for you if you find yourself frequently bypassing your own limits.

## Troubleshooting

### My blocks aren't active in the browser.
1.  Check the **Protection Health** card in Settings.
2.  Ensure your **NextDNS Profile ID** and **API Key** are entered correctly.
3.  Verify that the extension has permission to "Manage your apps" and "Modify network settings."

### Sync is failing.
1.  Check your internet connection.
2.  Ensure your NextDNS profile hasn't exceeded its monthly query limit.
3.  Perform a **Manual Push** from the Sync Diagnostics section in Settings.

### I can't disable a block!
If **Strict Mode** is on, you will see a countdown timer. You must wait for the timer to reach zero before the "Confirm" button becomes active. This is not a bug; it's a feature to support your long-term focus.

## Reference

- **Package Structure**:
  - `@focusgate/core`: Shared logic for rule evaluation and domain matching.
  - `@focusgate/sync`: Orchestration for NextDNS and multi-device state.
  - `@focusgate/state`: Persistence and hydration logic.
- **Enforcement Levels**:
  - **Level 1 (Local)**: Browser-only blocks.
  - **Level 2 (Hybrid)**: Local + NextDNS Denylist sync.
  - **Level 3 (Full)**: Local + NextDNS Services & Categories.
