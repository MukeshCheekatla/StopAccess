# Future Launch And Growth Plan
> Scope: turning FocusGate into a launchable, supportable, monetizable product

## Goal

Move from “it works for us” to “strangers can install, trust, recommend, and pay for it.”

## Launch Readiness

### Trust

- polished onboarding
- visible protection health
- privacy-first messaging
- clear explanation of NextDNS dependency

### Support

- troubleshooting docs
- in-app diagnostics
- known-issues page
- recovery flows for auth and sync failures

### Release Ops

- release checklist per platform
- versioning discipline
- smoke tests for Android and extension
- simple changelog process

## Growth Work

### Activation

- make first successful block happen in under 5 minutes
- add starter presets
- show a clear “protection active” success moment

### Retention

- daily summary loop
- streaks and behavior feedback
- saved time and focus wins
- reminder nudges when protection is off

### Monetization

- keep a strong free core
- possible paid features:
  stronger anti-bypass, advanced schedules, guardian controls, deeper insights, preset packs, history export
- do not gate core trust or setup behind paywalls

## Market Positioning

- not another passive screen-time tracker
- not a generic parental-control product
- a commitment device for adults who want real friction against distraction

## Suggested Milestones

1. trustworthy Android beta
2. polished extension beta
3. shared brand and site
4. pricing experiment
5. public launch
