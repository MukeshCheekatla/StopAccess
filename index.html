Privacy

Our Promise

StopAccess is built for your privacy. We do not use third-party analytics (like Google Analytics) or track your personal identity. Everything we do is designed to keep your data on your machine.

Data Usage

  • Browsing activity: We only look at the sites you visit to check if they should be blocked. This happens 100% locally.
  • Local Storage: Your block lists, PIN, and stats are stored safely on your own computer.
  • NextDNS Sync: If you connect a NextDNS profile, we only share the rules you choose so they work on all your devices.

Zero Tracking

We do not sell your browsing data, use it for ads, or send it to analytics companies. We only load icons from trusted providers to make your block list easy to read.

  • No history tracking servers
  • No IP address logging
  • No hidden analytics SDKs

Standard Permissions

We use standard browser features to protect you:

  • Storage: To keep your block lists and settings on your computer.
  • Blocking: To stop distracting sites from loading.
  • Tabs & Navigation: To detect which site you are on so we can block it if needed.
  • Timers & Idle: To keep your focus sessions running correctly, even when your computer is asleep.
  • Notifications: To alert you when your focus time is up.
  • Scripting: To show the "Access Denied" screen on blocked sites.
